What we do

Far from your standard software company, we have strong foundations in math and science that equip us to tackle challenging problems with precision and ingenuity. From algorithm design to data analysis, we dedicate ourselves to delivering cutting-edge software that solves hard problems accurately and fast, driving innovation and breakthroughs with state-of-the-art science.

Algorithms

Choosing an algorithm is like choosing a tool: using the right one is key to getting the best results. We can help you determine the critical components of your system and then select the right tools & algorithms for the job.

Analysis

Data tells a story, though often it can be difficult to read. With a wide array of analysis techniques from across disciplines at our disposal, we can help you to understand the story within your data and build the tools you need to read it.

Optimization

It's easy to optimize your code for a problem; it's far more difficult to optimize a problem for your code. Let us take a look at your system to help you get the most out of your math.

Communication

Communicating your results is as important as finding them. From articles to presentations to blog posts, we can help you to connect at any technical level.
